Schools

We believe in nurturing talent and exploring perspectives in our schools & communities.

Positive change

Bounce Culture aims to inspire positive change whilst introducing students to the benefits of the Creative Industries. We support former students continuing their studies in media courses in Further & Higher Education across the UK and Ireland. Many have gone onto work in the music and media industry. We offer GCSE Curriculum Design, Delivery & Examination Assistance, as well as Open College Network units. We have prepared students for GCSE in:

  • Foyle College,
  • Lumen Christi,
  • Oakgrove Integrated School,
  • St Columb’s College,
  • St Cecilia’s College,
  • Thornhill College,
  • St Joseph’s College,
  • St Peter’s School,
  • Coleraine College,
  • Ashfield Girls School,
  • Our Lady Of Mercy Girls School,
  • Newtonbreda High School,
  • St Dominic's Grammar School For Girls
  • St Malachy's College.
